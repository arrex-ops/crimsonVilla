Escape with us

Crimson Villa is a romantic yet family friendly self-contained home away from home with clean lines and a colourful aura. Fully equipped with the comforts and luxuries of home, Crimson villa sleeps up to 5 people, with a queen bed, two singles, plus a lounge/sofa.

Enjoy nature

Secluded above Catalina Reserve and surrounded by the Blue Mountains National Park we are close to a variety of bush walks. Along with a private spacious garden and with regular visits by Crimson Rosellas, King Parrots and Bower Birds, the villa is just the right distance from Katoomba village to provide both tranquility and convenience.

See the sites

The famous Three Sisters, Scenic Railway and local cafes and galleries are only minutes away. A visit to the newly refurbished Hydro Majestic at Mellow Bath is a 10 minute drive and well worth the short trip...Treat yourself and enjoy one of their High Teas..
